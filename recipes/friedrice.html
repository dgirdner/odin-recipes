Fried Rice Recipe

college Classic

This recipe dates back to my freshmen year of college at Northern Arizona University. A true college classic!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Pork
  • Bell peppers
  • Soy sauce
  • Teriyaki Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Fish Sauce
  • Penut butter

Steps

  1. Start Boiling 8 cups of water.
  2. When water boils add 4 cups of rice to the water and cook until water is gone.
  3. Chop up the pork and bell peppers and toss in a medium high heat pan with oil
  4. Add seasoning to the above combo
  5. Mix Soy sauce, terkiyaki sauce, Fish sauce, and penut butter in a small cup and heat in microwave for 30 seconds
  6. Add half the sauce mixture to the meat and pepper combo
  7. Turn on low until the rice is finished
  8. Add rice to the meat and peppers and mix around
  9. add the rest of the sacue mixture
  10. Tune stove on high heat to let the rice get cripy
  11. When crispy pushfood to the walls clearing room for eggs to touch the pan and add the eggs
  12. Cook eggs until scrabbled and then combine the mixture all together
  13. Enjoy
